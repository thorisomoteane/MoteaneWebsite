Cloud Migration

The "Zero-Downtime" Law Firm

30% Cost Saving

Infrastructure

The Logistics Warehouse Overhaul

Full Perimeter View
Dead Zones Eliminated

The Challenge: A warehouse in Midrand suffered from theft due to blind spots in their CCTV and had "dead zones" where scanners wouldn't connect to Wi-Fi.

The Moteane Solution: We installed a Hikvision IP Camera network with night vision and mobile alerts. We also re-cabled the facility with CAT6 and installed high-density Ubiquiti Access Points.
